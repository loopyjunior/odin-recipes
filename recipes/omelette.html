Omelette

Ingrediants

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tsbp Butter

Equipment

  • Frying pan
  • Whisk

Steps to prepare

  1. Beat and whisk the eggs in a bowl
  2. Add any seasoning you want pepper or salt
  3. Add the butter and oil into frying pan and heat them up
  4. Pour the eggs into the pan and slowly and tilt the pan in each direction to let the egss spread
  5. after 30 seconds flip the omelette with a spatulla
  6. Enjoy the food with any side you want.
