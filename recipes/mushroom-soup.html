Mushroom Soup

Ingrediants

  • 90g Butter
  • 2 Onions
  • 1 Garlic Glove
  • 500g Mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp plain flour
  • 4 tbsp single cream
  • Chick stock

Equipment

  • Saucepan
  • Blender / hand blender

Steps to prepare

  1. Chop up the garlic and onion.
  2. Heat up the butter on a saucepan and coik the onions and garlic until soft and not brown.
  3. Add muushrooms into the saucepan and cook over a high heat for 3 minitues untill soft.
  4. Add 2 tbsp of flour into the saucepan and mix well with the mushrooms
  5. Add chicken stock into the saucepan stir well and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. pour the saucepan into a blender and blend untill th mixture is smooth.
  7. Reheat the mixture and add creame.
