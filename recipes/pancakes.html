Pancakes

Ingrediants

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 300ml Milk
  • 2 tbsp sunflower oil

Equipment

  • Frying pan
  • Whisk or Hand Blender

Steps to prepare

  1. Add the eggs, milk and flour into a bowl and whisk together untill smooth.
  2. Set the frying pan to a medium heat and add some sunflower oil.
  3. Add the pancake batter into the frying pan.
  4. cook the pancakes for 1 minute on each side or untill golden brown.
  5. Enjoy the pancakes with any side you want.
